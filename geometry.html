Geometry Formulas

1. Perimeter of a Rectangle

Perimeter = 2(l + w)

Where l is the length and w is the width.

2. Area of a Rectangle

Area = l × w

Where l is the length and w is the width.

3. Perimeter of a Square

Perimeter = 4a

Where a is the side length.

4. Area of a Square

Area = a²

Where a is the side length.

5. Area of a Triangle

Area = ½ × b × h

Where b is the base and h is the height.

6. Area of a Triangle (Heron's Formula)

Area = √[s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c)]

Where s is the semi-perimeter and a, b, c are the side lengths.

7. Area of a Parallelogram

Area = b × h

Where b is the base and h is the height.

8. Area of a Trapezium (Trapezoid)

Area = ½ × (a + b) × h

Where a and b are the lengths of the parallel sides, and h is the height.

9. Area of a Circle

Area = πr²

Where r is the radius.

10. Circumference of a Circle

Circumference = 2πr

Where r is the radius.

11. Pythagorean Theorem

c² = a² + b²

In a right-angled triangle, where c is the hypotenuse, and a and b are the other two sides.

12. Volume of a Cube

Volume = a³

Where a is the side length.

13. Volume of a Rectangular Prism (Cuboid)

Volume = l × w × h

Where l is the length, w is the width, and h is the height.

14. Volume of a Cylinder

Volume = πr²h

Where r is the radius of the base, and h is the height.

15. Surface Area of a Sphere

Surface Area = 4πr²

Where r is the radius.

16. Volume of a Sphere

Volume = (4/3)πr³

Where r is the radius.

17. Surface Area of a Cone

Surface Area = πr(r + l)

Where r is the radius of the base, and l is the slant height.

18. Volume of a Cone

Volume = (1/3)πr²h

Where r is the radius of the base, and h is the height.

19. Surface Area of a Cylinder

Surface Area = 2πrh + 2πr²

Where r is the radius of the base, and h is the height.
