Probability Formulas

Basic Probability Formula

Probability of an event (A) happening:

P(A) = \(\frac{\text{Number of favorable outcomes}}{\text{Total number of outcomes}}\)

Complementary Probability

Probability of the event not happening (A'):

P(A') = 1 - P(A)

Addition Rule of Probability

For any two events A and B, the probability that A or B occurs (or both):

P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A ∩ B)

Multiplication Rule of Probability

For independent events A and B, the probability that both A and B occur:

P(A ∩ B) = P(A) × P(B)
