Key Formulas in Fractions

1. Addition of Fractions

Formula:
\( \frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{(a \times d) + (b \times c)}{b \times d} \)

Example:
\( \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{2} = \frac{(1 \times 2) + (4 \times 1)}{4 \times 2} = \frac{2 + 4}{8} = \frac{6}{8} = \frac{3}{4} \)

2. Subtraction of Fractions

Formula:
\( \frac{a}{b} - \frac{c}{d} = \frac{(a \times d) - (b \times c)}{b \times d} \)

Example:
\( \frac{3}{4} - \frac{1}{2} = \frac{(3 \times 2) - (4 \times 1)}{4 \times 2} = \frac{6 - 4}{8} = \frac{2}{8} = \frac{1}{4} \)

3. Multiplication of Fractions

Formula:
\( \frac{a}{b} \times \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a \times c}{b \times d} \)

Example:
\( \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{3}{4} = \frac{2 \times 3}{3 \times 4} = \frac{6}{12} = \frac{1}{2} \)

4. Division of Fractions

Formula:
\( \frac{a}{b} \div \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a}{b} \times \frac{d}{c} = \frac{a \times d}{b \times c} \)

Example:
\( \frac{3}{4} \div \frac{2}{3} = \frac{3}{4} \times \frac{3}{2} = \frac{3 \times 3}{4 \times 2} = \frac{9}{8} = 1 \frac{1}{8} \)

5. Simplifying Fractions

Formula:
\( \frac{a}{b} = \frac{a \div \text{GCD}}{b \div \text{GCD}} \)

Example:
\( \frac{12}{16} = \frac{12 \div 4}{16 \div 4} = \frac{3}{4} \)

6. Converting Mixed Numbers to Improper Fractions

Formula:
\( \text{Mixed Number} = n \frac{a}{b} \) converts to \( \frac{(n \times b) + a}{b} \)

Example:
\( 2 \frac{1}{3} = \frac{(2 \times 3) + 1}{3} = \frac{6 + 1}{3} = \frac{7}{3} \)

7. Converting Improper Fractions to Mixed Numbers

Formula:
\( \frac{a}{b} = n \frac{r}{b} \) where \( n \) is the quotient and \( r \) is the remainder.

Example:
\( \frac{7}{3} = 2 \frac{1}{3} \) (since 7 รท 3 = 2 with remainder 1)
